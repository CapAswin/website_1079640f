Frequently Asked Questions

01
What does the platform help brands do?

Brands can discover creators, launch campaigns, manage approvals, track deliverables, and monitor campaign performance from one place.

02
How do creators benefit from the service?

Creators gain direct visibility to brands, manage collaborations more independently, and present a stronger professional profile for paid opportunities.

03
Is the platform self-service or agency-managed?

The platform is designed as a self-service environment, giving both brands and creators more direct control over collaboration, communication, and execution.

04
Does verification play a role in the service?

Yes. Verification supports trust, improves quality control, and helps the platform maintain safer, more credible collaboration opportunities.

05
Can the platform support growth over time?

Yes. The service is built for ongoing growth, whether a brand is managing more campaigns or a creator is scaling visibility and monetization opportunities.
